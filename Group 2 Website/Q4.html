STREAMING ANALYSIS

SECOND STREAM

The live stream was just a simple presentation about cycling, including materials and reasons, as well as proof to support our claim. In addition, we still keep the vlog type after the presentation. Of coarse, we prepared a back up slide for audience to visualize the topic. The livestream started at 4 pm by april 15, and it ended at 5 pm. We then took the forms and collected audience feedback and more.

AUDIENCE

The stream lasted an hour and got a total of 100 viewers and 49 reactions. The engagement between audience is simple and it was fun for the group to not just share knowledge about cycling, nut also excited to know each and everyone's opinion. While the stream was going we continue to show the bikers stream and talk. There are things that we learn and things that missing on terms of ccoperating. As a group we acknowledge and give our best to improve the stream. Thanks to the feedback, so at this time the 5 brakes member opened their camera and together greeted the social media.

As a group, we know not all people can ride a bike, nor afford one. But encouraging others and spreading awareness can be accomplished as well. This is to let the people know what other small or simple things they can do to sustain nature and a healthy environment.
